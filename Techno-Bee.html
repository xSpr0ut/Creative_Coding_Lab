[ Techno-Bee ]

Scientific Name:

Techno-Apis

Discovered by / at:

A scientist who experimented on injured bees in 2032.

Family:

Bees, Apoidea

Habitat:

Sunny, grassy, and flower-dense areas; warm climate; gardens & meadows, particularly near cities.

Appearance:

Appears like a regular bee but with a different coloured coating, an indicator of the bee’s energy level

Key Characteristics:

  • Flies.
  • Naturally attracted to flowers to pollinate.
  • Need to be charged regularly
  • Leaves behind a trail of small fumes

What we know so far about Techno-Bee? A subspecies of bee which run on technological advancements. Although not subjected to the life span of a regular bee, it requires regular upkeep (charging) and leaves behind a trail of small fumes. Wanders around collecting pollen, but it will require charging after two rounds of pollination.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature Using your mouse, hold a flower near techno-bees. They will naturally gravitate towards you unless they have recently pollinated a flower or are low on battery. It will follow the flower endlessly until it has landed on the flower for long enough to pollinate it.
